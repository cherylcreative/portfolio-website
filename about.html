About

My Story

I am a Singapore based Project Manager and UX designer who has a passion for organisation and creating flows. I love learning about people, the way they think, work and act. This helps me hone the ability to observe and ask the right questions which often brought insights to improve workflows and user flows. Ultimately, be more efficient. For a team or a user.

I was a science student growing up which I believe attributed to my logical sense of thinking, and exposing myself to a fine arts minor in my university exchange had changed my whole perception of what design can do. Since then, I had pursued two different courses - graphic design and UX design which gave me the opportunity to work in e-commerce companies as a UI/UX designer.

During my time at a startup, I figured that my passion lies with organisation, productivity and structure when I felt the urge to help out in these areas besides UI and UX. Seeing that opportunity, I had proposed the value I could bring and now wear multiple hats overseeing the business as a Project Manager, in charge of cafe, retail, event, content and website.

I love learning and I forever will. I'm always interested to pick up a new skill (mostly crafts) - my latest, sewing. I love to explore nature and hike occasionally hike or head to the beach. My day-to-day, I try to keep to my routine to read, journal and practice yoga. On non-productive days, you'll find me binge watching dramas on netflix.
